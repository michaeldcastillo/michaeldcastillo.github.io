I strive to bridge the gap between business and technology and only find technology interesting when it solves business problems and serves the needs of real people. As such, user experience provides the context for how I think about every product or service created and consumed.

Most recently, I have spent a 10 years of my career learning and working with Microsoft SharePoint and related technologies (now Office 365) to facilitate team collaboration and communication in the workplace. Prior to that, I have spent many additional years working as a designer for print and digital media (branding, user interface, audio editing, video editing, web animation, etcetera).
